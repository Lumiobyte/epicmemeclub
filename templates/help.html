Uploading Rules

We'd like to keep our site a fun and accesible place for all ages to find and share their favourite memes. This means you CANNOT upload NSFW, sexual, gory, or otherwise unsuitable content.

Please do not upload any content that could offend others. Once again, in an effort to keep this website and its content universally accessible, you CANNOT upload any content that could hurt or offend others. Even if you think it's a joke or that it's funny, others could take it to heart.

This website is not free advertising space! Please DO NOT upload content that is solely for the purpose of promoting you/your business/another entity or content that is devoid of substance and does not deserve a few megabytes on our server. We have limited resources, none of which can be used to store spam!

Watermarks on your original content is excluded, but do not make them exaggerated, or have them cover the content.
